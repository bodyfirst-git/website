More about Sandeep Gupta

Mr. Sandeep Gupta is a distinguished figure in the Nutrition and Nutraceutical industry, holding roles such as Nutraceutical Evangelist, Global Speaker, Executive Coach, and Chief Mentor. With a background in pharmaceuticals and an MBA, he is associated with Leadership Excellence at Harvard Square, USA. Trained by John Mattone, a coach of Steve Jobs, he is a certified Intelligent Leadership Coach.

Gupta, with over 23 years of experience, excels in Self-Leadership, Servant Leadership, and Intelligent Leadership Practices. He mentors young professionals, emphasizing catching them young and instilling passion. His vast network includes senior executives, scientists, expats, authors, and coaches globally.

Beyond his professional achievements, Gupta is a Fitness & Nutritional Food enthusiast, with interests in cricket, chess, and media analysis. His leadership has shaped Geltec & Universal Medicare, the largest Nutraceutical manufacturer. Gupta leads seven functions and is a go-to for MNCs, Indian corporations, and startups.

As Chief Founder of the Expert Nutraceutical Advocacy Council (ENAC), he focuses on Nutraceutical sector growth through education and networking. Leading associations like IDMA and CoSGM, he's also a National Task Force member for CII & FICCI. Gupta chairs the Standard Review Group for Nutraceuticals at FSSAI and serves on the Advisory Board of UBM.

Recent achievements include being the only Indian on the Executive Advisory Board at Informa Exhibitions, a Panelist Judge for Nutraceutical Ingredients Asia Awards, and recognition among the top 100 global achievers. Gupta is an active National Spokesperson for the Health Care & Food Industry in India and internationally.
